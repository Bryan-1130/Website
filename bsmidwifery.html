Bachelor of Science in Midwifery

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Midwifery (BSM) is a four-year ladderized degree program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to provide comprehensive care to women throughout the childbearing cycle, including prenatal, childbirth, postpartum, and newborn care.

This program combines general education with professional courses and extensive clinical training to prepare students for licensure as registered midwives.
