About BSIT

The Bachelor Of Science In Information Technology (BSIT) is a 4-years degree program that prepares students to be IT PROFESSIONALS who are able to perform installation, Operation, Development, Maintenance, And Administration of computer Applications. The goal of the program is to gear up students as "INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIST" Who can assist individuals and organizations in solving prolems using information technologies (IT) Techniques

  • Web and Application Developer
  • Junior Database Administrator
  • System Administrator

  • Junior Information Security Administrator
  • System Integration Personel
  • IT Audit Assistant
  • Technical Support Specialist

    Secondary Job:

  • QA Specialist
  • System Analyst
  • Computer Programmer

About ACT

The Associate in Computer Technology or (ACT) is a 2-years degree program that provides specialized computing and information technology tracks equipping graduates with a required skills and compitencies catering to the needs of industry. The curriculumn covers rule based learning where students will use industry-based content, be able to earn certifications work with industry-served hands on excercises using latest technologies and be maintained by highly qualified faculty and industry practitioners. By completing an industry-based project, students apply and extend their knowledge and understanding by exploring them to work in a professional environment. While the majority of students work is done on independent basis, there are opportunities to develop teamwork skills with group-based projects throughout the program.
