Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM) is a four-year degree program that covers the process planning, development, and human resource management of various aspects of hotel, restaurant, and resort operations. The program also intends to teach entrepreneurship skills.

"Embark on a Journey to Success with our Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management Course at Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology Inc. ! Join our exclusive tie-up with TO GO Travel for an onboard apprenticeship training experience like no other. Start your career in hospitality management with hands-on learning and real-world opportunities."
